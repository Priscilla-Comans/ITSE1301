Lab Two Part B

Lab-Two part B required us to use Photoshop Quick Fixes. For this lab, I had to use Content-Aware Fill or the Clone Tool. I chose a picture of two dogs for this project. I removed my friend's dog from the image and only kept my dog.


The steps that I used to fix this image were the following:

  1. I used the clone stamp to clone a section of the carpet.
  2. I used what was cloned to cover up the unwanted visitor from the image.
