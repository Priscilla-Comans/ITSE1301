Lab Three Part B

The main focus of Lab-Four-Part-Three was learning how to layer images and use the layer blend tool correctly. This lab required that we use two images and blend them to create a new overall picture. The idea for this lab was for the picture to have a "Ghostly" vibe. I chose to work with two images of my hometown. The first picture is an old abandoned train station, and the second one is a picture of the Canyon night sky. Once layered and blended, the images created a spooky train station.


The steps that I used to fix this image were the following:

  1. I set the building picture as the first layer and the landscape picture as layer two.
  2. Resized both images to 800 pixel for width and height.
  3. Used the "Color Burn" effect to blend the images together.
