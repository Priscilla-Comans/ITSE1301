Lab Four Part C

The purpose of Lab Four, Part C, was to correct the Color-Tint or to Change the Color-Hue of an image. For this lab, I chose a picture of my son that had an overwhelming blue color-tint. The lab required that I utilized the Hue/Saturation tool on Photoshop. This lab was my favorite!!


The steps that I used to fix this image were the following:

  1. I Resized image to 800 pixel in width and height.
  2. Used color balance to adjust the midtones, highlights, and shadows. midtones were set to; reds +83, magenta +38, and yellow -52. highlights were set to; reds +34, magenta +18, and yellow -18. shadows were set to; reds +17, magenta -10, and yellow -17.
